Harshitha's Recipe for making tasty and Healthy Carom Rice !!!

Ingredients

Must Have

  • 2 teaspoon's of Carom seeds
  • 200 grams Rice
  • 10 grams Oil
  • 5-6 Cloves
  • 4 Dried Red Chillies
  • 1 teaspoon Tampering Seeds
  • 2 stards Curry Leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric
  • Salt as per taste

Optional Items

  • Onions
  • Chillies
  • Peanuts
  • Sesame Oil

Making process

  1. First, Heat the Pan and add oil
  2. Once the oil turn hot, Add tampered seeds, cloves and dried red chillies
  3. Now, Wait for 2 minutes, before adding our hero ingregient Carom seeds
  4. Again fry the Carom seed in the pan for a minute and add Rice into it
  5. Next, add Salt and turmeric(optional)
  6. Let it cook for another minute and turn off the stove
  7. Serve it Hot, And Enjoy:)

!! Tasty and Healty Carom Rice is ready to Serve. Enjoy it on a Rainy Day for Utmost Satisfaction !!
